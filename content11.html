IKEA Redesign

Website Redesign





IKEA Redesign is a side project that I finished recently. Apparently the current design of IKEA has a series of problems like unorganized information, inconsistent typography (including texts within images), etc. My work involves two pages, the homepage and the product page, that are focusing on building trustworthy, engaging and pleasant shopping experience through organized, minimal and consistent design.
