The Scotts

Character Design | Graphic Design





The Scotts is a set of cartoon characters design which included Scott's family members, various jobs of Scott and Scotts on movie screens. In each section of characters, I focused on how minor features could affect the recongnizability of characters, aka. how to merge existing roles with the targeting roles.










In this section, I drew four family members of Scott including his dad, younger sister, younger brother and his grandfather.







In this section, I create a set of characters of animated sitcoms and marvel comics including Detective Conan, The Smurfs, Ninja Turtles, One Piece, Naruto, Superman, Captain America, Fantastic Four, Spider Man and Batman.








In the last section, I designed a set of Scotts for different jobs. Jobs involve with magician, policeman, chief, school boy, Chinese traditional tutor, doctor, and Japanese ninja, etc.
